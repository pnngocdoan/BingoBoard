Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Bingo Z ("the app") is a bingo board generator app built for personal use and entertainment. This policy explains what data is involved when you use the app.

1. Data We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our own servers.

2. AI Suggestions (Google Gemini)

When you use the AI suggestion feature, the text you type into bingo cells is sent to Google Gemini API to generate suggestions. This means:

  • Your input text is processed by Google's servers.
  • We do not store your prompts or the AI responses.
  • Google may process this data according to their own policies.

Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API data.

3. No Account Required

The app requires no sign-up, login, or account. No personal identifiers are collected.

4. Local Storage

Bingo board data (cell text, game state) is stored only on your device using local storage. It is never uploaded to any server.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
