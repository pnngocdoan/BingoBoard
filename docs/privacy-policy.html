Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Bingo Z ("the app") is a bingo board generator app. This policy explains what data is involved when you use the app.

1. Age Requirement

Bingo Z is intended for users 13 years of age or older. By using the app, you confirm that you meet this requirement. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

2. Data We Store

Your bingo board content (cell text, game state) is stored in Supabase, a cloud database, to enable persistence across sessions. This data is associated with your board but not with any personal identity unless you choose to create an account.

3. AI Suggestions (Groq)

When you use the AI suggestion feature, the text you type into bingo cells is sent to Groq API to generate suggestions. This means:

  • Your input text is processed by Groq's servers.
  • We do not store your prompts or the AI responses beyond the session.
  • Groq may process this data according to their own policies.

Please review Groq's Privacy Policy for details.

4. No Account Required

The app requires no sign-up or login by default. No personal identifiers are collected unless you choose to sign in.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — board storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Groq — AI suggestions. Groq Privacy Policy

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
